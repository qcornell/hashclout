Download HashPack Wallet

First, download the HashPack wallet browser extension or mobile app if you don't already have it. HashPack is a secure wallet for the Hedera ecosystem.

2
Associate the CLOUT Token

Open your HashPack wallet and associate the CLOUT token using its token ID:

0.0.4903185

To associate a token, go to the "Tokens" tab in your wallet, click "Associate Token," and enter the token ID.

3
Swap HBAR for CLOUT

After associating the token, you can swap your HBAR for CLOUT tokens through a decentralized exchange like HeliSwap or Saucerswap that lists the CLOUT/HBAR pair.

4
Connect Your Wallet to HashClout

After purchasing CLOUT tokens, connect your HashPack wallet to the HashClout platform to enjoy the benefits of holding CLOUT.
